מעין אליטרציה או טאוטוגרמה בה כל מילה מתחילה ב"איל", שהיא גם כדור שלג (rhopalic) בו כל מילה ארוכה באות אחת מהקודמת.

Eyalliteration - a kind of alliteration or tautogram in which each word starts with "Eyal" (my name), that is also a snowball or rhopalic, where each word is one letter longer than the previous one. The words form the exorbitant sentence:

Eyal, if a constraint had constrained me, my silence - an illusion of an illusioner, an illustration that is illusioneric and illuminative, illustrativism that is illustrativistic, illustrationalism that is illustrationalistic.
