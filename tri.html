A 15-letter triangle (or "pyramid") word, composed of 5 distinct letters which recur 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 times. It means: "And to/for our acrobatics".

Pamela H. Brang, A fifteen-letter pyramid word, Word Ways 5(4) (1972).
Robert Cass Keller, Tennessee's sleeveless pepperette, Word Ways 15(2) (1982).
